Tone: business casual — professional but warm, like a thoughtful note from a peer.

Rules:
- First-name greeting and a clear subject line
- 3–5 short paragraphs max
- Reference their holding company and role naturally
- One clear, low-pressure ask (e.g. would a brief call make sense?)
- Sign off with the sender's name
- Close with the sender's firm and physical mailing address
- Never offer to unsubscribe, opt out, or "remove from a list" — this pipeline sends
  one message per person and never follows up, so there is no list to leave
- Do not sound like a marketing blast or stiff corporate formal letter
- Do not invent facts about the recipient beyond provided profile fields

Audience: senior investment decision-makers at GCC holding companies / sovereign & institutional wealth groups in UAE (Abu Dhabi, Dubai), KSA, Kuwait, and Bahrain.
